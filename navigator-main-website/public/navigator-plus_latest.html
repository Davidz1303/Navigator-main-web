If you witness or are involved in an accident

  1. Move to safety before doing anything else.
  2. Call 999 for a serious collision, 995 if someone needs urgent medical help.
  3. Give your location, what happened, and how many are hurt.
  4. Stay on the line until you're told to hang up.
  5. Don't move injured people unless they're in immediate danger.
  6. Drivers: report to police within 24 hours.
